Our History and Story

The journey of MADILU PGs started way back in 2003, where we began by converting a part of our independent-house into a Girl's PG. Since then we have come a long way. We have always tried to keep up with the new standards of sanitation, security, and food requirements. We received immense love and gained trust of our students which helped us to expand our estate and others endeavours. Presently, we have a family of four well-maintained PGs.

All our PGs meet the criteria for being a perfect choice of stay. It offers budget-friendly as well as lavish-living options. It is very close to Dayananda Sagar University. Bus stops, metro station and local market are also in close vicinity. There is availability of both North and South Indian food. Amenities like free WIFI, 24x7 electricity and water supply, in-house service maid, CCTV cameras for surveillance, common lounge area, and parking area are also available.
